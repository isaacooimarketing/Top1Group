# Calendar Profit Heatmap Design

The Grab calendar will encode each finished day's net profit using a fixed color tier. This makes strong and weak days recognizable before reading the number.

## Profit Tiers

- Below RM0: red
- RM0-RM99.99: neutral graphite/white
- RM100-RM149.99: light blue
- RM150-RM199.99: deeper blue
- RM200-RM249.99: light cyan
- RM250-RM299.99: deeper cyan
- RM300 and above: champagne gold

The tier color applies to the net-profit number, the left accent, border, and a restrained background tint. Platform remains visible in the status text. Rest and in-progress states retain their existing behavior.

The same tiers and contrast apply on desktop and mobile.
